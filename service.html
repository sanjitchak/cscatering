Our Services

Food, Decor & Entertainment

Food Catering

We take pride in offering exceptional food catering services, tailored to meet your event’s needs. Our chefs create delicious, high-quality dishes using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, ensuring every bite is a delight. Whether it's a grand wedding, corporate event, or private gathering, we design diverse menus that cater to various cuisines, dietary preferences, and themes. From live food stations to lavish buffets, we promise an unforgettable dining experience.

Wedding Decor

Make your wedding day truly magical with our bespoke wedding decor services. We specialize in creating elegant, personalized setups — from enchanting mandaps and floral arrangements to luxurious seating and lighting. Our team works closely with you to bring your vision to life, blending cultural traditions with modern design elements to create an ambiance that reflects your unique love story.

Theme Decor

Turn your event into an immersive experience with our creative theme decor services. Whether you dream of a royal affair, a whimsical garden, a boho-chic party, or a Bollywood-inspired bash, we design every detail to match your chosen theme. From custom backdrops and props to unique table settings and lighting, we make sure every corner of your venue tells a story.

DJ Setup

Get the party started with our professional DJ setups! We provide high-quality sound systems, vibrant lighting, LED dance floors, and experienced DJs who know how to read the crowd and keep energy levels high. Whether you want the latest chart-toppers, classic hits, or a custom playlist, we create a musical atmosphere that keeps your guests dancing all night long.

Bar Setup

Elevate your event with our premium bar setup services. We offer sleek, stylish bars stocked with a variety of beverages — from classic cocktails and mocktails to fine wines and spirits. Our skilled bartenders craft drinks to perfection, while the setup itself adds a touch of sophistication to your event. We can even create signature cocktails tailored to your event’s theme for a personalized touch.

Sheesha Catering

Add a touch of luxury and relaxation to your event with our hookah catering services. We provide high-quality hookah setups with a wide selection of flavors, premium equipment, and attentive staff to ensure everything runs smoothly. Whether it’s a wedding, birthday, or private party, our hookah lounge experience adds a unique and enjoyable element for your guests to unwind and socialise.
